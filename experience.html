My Biography

ESAF PUBG Mobile

My experience has represented the faculty in an esports event in the pubg mobile category. that was the first time I participated in an esports competition in UiTM. My friends and I made it to the quarter finals. The achievement is a high achievement for me despite being eliminated at the quarter-final stage. I can't forget this experience because I was able to represent my faculty.

Community Service Program

In the 3rd semester, my friend and I participated in a community service program. We held a beach cleaning program in Serabang Bay with the villagers. This program emphasizes the priority of cleanliness on the beach and re-instills a sense of responsibility in oneself to take care of the beach and other environments. The activities held are cleaning the beach, doing leisure activities with the villagers and having dinner with the community.
